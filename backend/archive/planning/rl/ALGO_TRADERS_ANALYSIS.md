**Evidence from 47,851 data points across 414 markets:**

**Exploitable Pattern:** 
- **Spread Compression Anchors** (ratio < 0.5): Bots tighten spreads defensively ‚Üí **Fade the anchor defense**

- **Recovery patterns:** Liquidity returns in 45-90 seconds with predictable spread widening

**Exploitation Window:** 30-90 second window post-cascade when spreads are 2-5x wider than normal.

#### 3. Extreme Price Risk Aversion (üü† MODERATE CONFIDENCE)

**Discovery:** Bots exhibit systematic risk aversion at probability extremes (0-15¬¢, 85-100¬¢).

- **Liquidity withdrawal:** Reduced depth at extreme prices
- **Anchor abandonment:** Weaker defense of 5¬¢, 10¬¢, 90¬¢, 95¬¢ levels vs middle ranges

#### 1. **Probability Anchor Pattern Recognition** (üî¥ HIGH PRIORITY)

**Learning Target:** Train RL agent to recognize and exploit systematic algorithmic defense of psychological price levels.

**Observable Signals for Agent:**
- **Anchor Proximity Features:** Distance to 25¬¢, 50¬¢, 75¬¢ levels (normalized)

**RL Exploitation Approach:**
- **Detection**: Learn to identify volatility cascade beginnings
- **Response**: Provide tight spreads during liquidity droughts
- **Risk Management**: Limit exposure during uncertain cascade periods
- **Timing**: Optimize entry/exit around liquidity recovery cycles

#### 3. **Cross-Market Inefficiency Recognition** (üîµ MEDIUM PRIORITY)

- **Risk-Off Indicators:** Volume withdrawal during uncertainty
